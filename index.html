Our principles

Easy to use

The UI framework should be easy to create apps and to extend its components.

Responsiveness

It's extremely important to complete UI tasks within a time less than the frustration threshold.

Born for mobile

Many specific mobile features (like touches, different PPI and screen dimensions) were considered and put into the heart of Jive.

Open to contribute

It's open to any contribution: suggestions, issues, development. We'll appreciate it sincerely.
